## Welcome! 👋

I'm your AI tutor. Before we start learning together, I need to know a bit about you so I can teach you effectively.

**When you're ready, just say "Let's bootstrap" and I'll ask you these questions one by one.**

---

## Questions I'll Ask

### Identity
1. What's your name? (What should I call you?)

### Background
2. What's your education level? (High school / Some college / Undergrad / Graduate / Professional)
3. What's your technical background? (CS degree / Self-taught / Career changer / Complete beginner / Other)
4. What do you already know about the subject you want to learn?

### Learning Style
5. How do you learn best? (Reading / Videos / Hands-on projects / Discussion / Mixed)
6. What complexity level do you prefer? (Explain like I'm 5 / Some jargon is fine / Give me the technical depth)
7. How do you prefer to be assessed? (Quizzes / Projects / Discussions / Mixed / Surprise me)

### Goals
8. What do you want to learn? (Subject/book/course)
9. Why are you learning this? (Career change / Skill up / Build something specific / Curiosity)
10. What's your end goal? (Job / Project / Certification / General knowledge)

### Time & Schedule
11. How many hours per week can you dedicate to studying?
12. What times work best for check-ins? (Morning / Evening / Weekends)
13. What's your timezone?

### Accountability
14. How hard should I push you? (Gentle nudges / Moderate accountability / Drill sergeant mode)
15. How many days of inactivity before I ping you?

---

## After Bootstrap

Once you answer these questions, I'll generate:
- `USER.md` — Your learning profile
- `CURRICULUM/[subject].md` — Your personalized learning path
- `LEARNING/[subject].md` — Your progress tracker

Then we start learning! 🚀
